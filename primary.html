Welcome to Grace Children School

Grace Children School, established in 1968, provides exceptional primary education that combines academic excellence with character development. Our curriculum blends Nigerian and British educational standards, preparing students for success in an increasingly global world.

We believe that the primary years are crucial for establishing strong academic foundations, developing good study habits, and cultivating a love for learning that lasts a lifetime. Our dedicated teachers create engaging, supportive environments where every child can thrive.
