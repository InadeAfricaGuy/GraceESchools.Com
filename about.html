About Grace E. Schools

A Legacy of Educational Excellence Since 1968

Our Story

Grace E. Schools was founded in 1968 by Deaconess Mrs. Grace Bisola Oshinowo with a vision to provide quality education that nurtures the whole child - academically, morally, and spiritually. What began as Grace Children School has grown into a comprehensive educational institution serving students from infancy through secondary education.

In 1994, Grace High School was established to extend our commitment to excellence into the secondary education level. Today, Grace E. Schools stands as one of the most respected private educational institutions in Lagos, Nigeria, with a proud tradition of producing graduates who excel both nationally and internationally.

Our Mission

To provide a holistic education that develops students academically, morally, spiritually, and socially, preparing them to become responsible leaders and productive citizens in a global society.

Our Vision

To be the leading educational institution in Nigeria, recognized for academic excellence, character development, and the production of godly students who are future leaders.

Our Philosophy

We believe that every child is unique and deserves an education that recognizes their individual talents, nurtures their potential, and builds their confidence to succeed.
