Welcome to Grace High School

Founded in 1994, Grace High School continues the legacy of academic excellence established by Grace Children School. We provide comprehensive secondary education that prepares students for success in higher education and beyond.

Our high school combines rigorous academics with character development, leadership training, and spiritual growth. With both day and boarding options available, we offer flexible learning environments that meet the diverse needs of our students and their families.
