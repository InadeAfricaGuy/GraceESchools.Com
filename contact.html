Frequently Asked Questions

When does the academic year start?

Our academic year typically begins in September and runs through July, following the Nigerian academic calendar.

Do you offer scholarships?

We offer merit-based scholarships and financial aid programs. Please contact our admissions office for details.

What is your student-teacher ratio?

We maintain small class sizes to ensure individual attention, with an average ratio of 1:20 depending on the level.

Do you provide transportation?

Yes, we offer school bus services covering various routes across Lagos. Contact us for route information.

Can I schedule a campus visit?

Absolutely! We encourage prospective parents to visit our campus. Please call or email to schedule a tour.

What documents are needed for admission?

Requirements vary by level but typically include birth certificate, previous academic records, medical records, and passport photographs.
